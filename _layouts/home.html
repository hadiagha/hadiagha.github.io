Featured Book: Reinforcement Learning for Business

Reinforcement Learning for Business shows how to take RL from theory to practice for real-world problems such as pricing, logistics, resource allocation, and operations. The book is designed for practitioners and decision-makers who want end-to-end, production-minded workflows rather than purely academic examples.

You will see how to frame business problems as RL tasks, integrate RL with forecasting and optimization, and deploy solutions on modern ML platforms.
